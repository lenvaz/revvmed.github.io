Our Services

Patient Pre-authorization

Initiating the revenue cycle with pre-authorization ensures smooth billing process, minimizing delays and denials down the line.

Insurance Eligibility & Verification

Insurance eligibility verification is the most important and the first step in the medical billing process. Revvmed's dedicated insurance eligibility verification team delivers a thorough verification, thereby aiding dramatic reduction of the claims' accounts receivable cycle.

Medical Billing

Our experts handle the entire claims submission process, from accurate coding to timely submissions, optimizing your reimbursement potential.

ERA/EOR Payment Posting

Payment posting is vital to the revenue cycle. At Revvmed, our expert team of payment posting system helps identify opportunities to increase the bottom line by creating daily inputs in your organization.

Denial management is all about figuring out why insurance claims get denied, fixing those issues, and preventing them from happening again. Our dedicated denial management team works on denials promptly, maximizing revenue collection.

Reporting
